Adamu Writes Senate, Replaces Abdullahi with Gobir As Senate Leader

Print
Category: News News

The majority All Progressives Congress (APC) on Wednesday nominated Senator Ibrahim Gobir (APC Sokoto East) as the new Senate Leader and Chairman of the APC caucus in the Senate.


In a letter to the Senate President, Ahmad Lawan, APC National Chairman, Abdullahi Adamu, asked that Gobir replace the former Senate Leader, Yahaya Abdullahi (Kebbi North), who defected from the ruling party to the People’s Democratic Party (PDP).

The letter reads, “With reference to your letter No.: NASS9thS/SP/D/12/ dated 16th June, 2022, in which you informed the party of the vacancy of the office of the Senate Leader of our party.

“Accept herewith, the party’s nomination of Senator Ibrahim Gobir (Sokoto East Senatorial District) as a replacement for the Senate Leader and Chairman of the APC Caucus.

“While thanking you for the usual cooperation, please accept the assurances of our best regards.”
