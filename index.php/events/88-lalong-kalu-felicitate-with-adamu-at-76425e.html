Lalong, Kalu felicitate with Adamu at 76

Print
Category: News News

Governor Simon Bako Lalong of Plateau State has felicitated with APC National Chairman, His Excellency Sen. Abdullahi Adamu on his 76th birthday, describing him as a gift to Nigeria.
Lalong in a congratulatory message, described him as a man of vast experience in politics and governance as well as a patriotic Nigerian who has contributed immensely to the development of the nation.

He said “Senator Adamu is one of Nigeria’s finest politicians who has deployed his experience, capacity and vast knowledge in building democratic institutions as well as mentoring the younger generation for the task of service and dedication to national ideals.

His long political career has been very valuable in the democratic journey of the nation as he played various roles at the Local, State and national levels effectively”.

According to Governor Lalong, the APC National Chairman since taking the mantle of leadership of the ruling party, has brought in stability, understanding and push for robust reconciliation which is repositioning it for effective service to Nigerians especially ahead of the 2023 general elections.

Governor Lalong noted that Senator. Adamu has also done a lot to boost the nation’s agriculture by engaging in farming and also contributing in the passage of various legislations that have boosted food production in the country during his term at the National Assembly where he headed the Senate Committee on Agriculture.

While wishing him many more years of good health, God’s blessings and divine protection, Lalong urged him to continue to serve the nation and also grooming younger ones towards making Nigeria great.

Also, the Chief Whip of the Senate, Dr Orji Kalu has described former Governor of Nasarawa State and National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Senator Abdullahi Adamu as a selfless statesman and consummate politician with passion for a united and indivisible Nigeria.

Acknowledging the contributions of the former governor to the social, economic and political development of Nigeria, Kalu, former governor of Abia State, stressed that the National Chairman of the APC is a rallying point for his contemporaries and the younger generation.

Kalu made these remarks in a goodwill message in commemoration of the 76th birthday anniversary of Adamu.

He said: “I congratulate former Governor of Nasarawa and National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC) as he clocks 76. The National Chairman has over the years built and sustained relationships across the nooks and crannies of Nigeria owing to his goodwill. His contributions to nation building in different capacities are worthy of commendation and emulation.

“The political class will continue to tap from the vast experience of the celebrant. “In his capacity as National Chairman of the APC, the party has been waxing strong in view of his leadership qualities. I celebrate the accomplishments of Turakin Keffi in all folds.”
