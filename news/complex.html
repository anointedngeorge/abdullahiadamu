NASARAWA GOVERNOR MOVES TO NEW COMPLEX

Nasarawa State Government has finally moved its seat of power to the new ultra-modern Government House complex along shendam road, Lafia.  Already, the State Governor, Alhaji Abdullahi Adamu and his family have moved into the multi-million Naira office complex that also serves as residence for the first family.  Government House sources report that most principal officers including the Permanent Secretary, Webmaster, Directors and other key officers have fully moved or were in the process of moving to the new office which is about three kilometers from the town.

The situation in the new Government house since the movement has been business-like with increasing number of people coming in either for official, private or sightseeing purposes.  Among the early callers to the new Government House was the Speaker of the State House of Assembly, the State Chairman of the ruling Party, PDP, as well as other top Government functionaries.  Top among the early events that took place at the new office was the Stateï¿½s Executive Council meeting, which was held at the conference hall of the complex.

Already, the complex is fitted with the necessary basic requirements such as Air-conditioners, electric gadgets as well as furniture and other basic equipment befitting the complex, which is described as an architectural masterpiece.

The new Government House which is described as a masterpiece consists of office and residential house building; two 3-bedroom guest houses, a clinic and four sets 2-bedroom domestic staff quarters.  Also in the complex is a tennis court and swimming pool.
