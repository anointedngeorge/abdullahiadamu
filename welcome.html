WELCOME MESSAGE

It is my pleasure to welcome you to my virtual office on the Internet. Valuable information about my service to the people of Nasarawa State will now be available to web users around the world.

Nasarawa State contains the largest deposits of solid minerals in Nigeria. Though a relatively new state, it is one of the country�s most innovative and forward looking. In the short period of my service as Governor and chief servant of the state, we have shown a strong commitment to the use of modern information technology to change the destiny of our people. First we placed the whole state and its resources on the Internet, and now, my office is being opened up to all Internet users around the world. The intent is to uphold the tenets of transparency and accountability in governance.

If you are an indigene of Nasarawa State, I hope the information posted here will help you better understand the work of your governor. If you�re one of our friends, we hope that this site will give you a unique view of our great state and its government. Included in this site are public policy issues, my speeches as governor, my various activities and a photo profile. A link takes you to our main Nasarawa State site.

Our goal in Nasarawa State is to do more than just be a part of the technology of the new millennium, we aspire to be known as the information technology state of Nigeria. We recognize the power of the information superhighway and plan to make the most of its boundless potential for our state.

Information is power. Democracy works best when citizens are well informed of the activities of their elected leaders.

Feel free to contact me of any issues of interest to you. My virtual door is open permanently.

Thank you for visiting us. Happy surfing.

Alhaji Abdullahi Adamu
(Sarkin Yakin Keffi)
Executive Governor, Nasarawa State.
