Welcome Message

It is a pleasure to welcome you to my website through which I intend to interact with you about my work as the National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress, APC. I consider it important in the course of my service to the APC to interface with all members of the Party, Nigerians and indeed, the international community. In that connection, I will, through this website, provide you with details of my work as Chairman, my ideas on the policies and programmes of the party and much of the information you require to stay connected with me and our party.

All Progressives Congress is a broad-based political party that is focused on providing a strong development platform for Nigeria. Our party has a deep-rooted community presence in the country and believes in creating opportunities for the private sector to contribute to the development of Nigeria.

I urge you to constantly visit this site to interface with me via email and other social media platforms, on various issues affecting our dear country Nigeria.

The APC Will Win In A Fair Contest - Adamu

  • Print
  • Email
Category: News News
Published: 16 June 2022 16 June 2022

The National Chairman of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Senator Abdullahi Adamu has said that the APC was sure to win the 2023 general elections which will be held in a fair and just manner. He made this assertion while receiving the Ambassador of Poland to Nigeria, Joanna Tarnawska in his office. The chairman stated that with the massive support the party enjoyed from over 43 million Nigerians, there was no way the party will lose the 2023 elections.

The Polish Ambassador who was visiting the Chairman after the successful special national convention which produced a presidential flagbearer for the party, had earlier congratulated him on his election as chairman. She stated that her country was interested in political developments within Nigeria and will like to see a peaceful transition to another president after the tenure of President Buhari ends next year. She also called for the APC to create opportunities for women and young persons to participate in the political terrain.
